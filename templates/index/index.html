Our Vision

KVVS College of Science & Technology strives for excellence in higher education, research and support services, by employing an integral approach to education, with the purpose of forming professionals, responsible citizens and epitomes of fundamental human values like commitment, compassion and optimism, who ought to be actively involved in creative nation building and enhancement of human existence.

Our Mission

To transform young men and women into professionally competent, socially committed and morally upright individuals who are well equipped for outstanding performance in the application of human resources and potentialities on a global dimension and are empowered to serve humanity impelled by their unwavering commitment to the ultimate values of human existence.
